Anthom

Address: 9 Clinton Street
New York, NY 10002
Website: http://www.shopanthom.com/

ANTHOM is an online + brick and mortar women's clothing store that caters to thoughtful women who appreciate design, quality, and uniqueness. It takes a measured approach to retail and offer clothing that women can live in and subtly stand out.
The owners believe the modern woman dresses "high/low" combining affordable items with investment pieces intended to last a lifetime; this philosophy is reflected in the range of prices and designers offered in their store.

In Support Of

Address: 342 W 13th Street
New York, NY 10014
Website: http://www.insupportof.us/

Founded in 2014 by Tanya Sheikh & Ivan Gilkes, In Support Of is a retail concept with the goal of bringing inspiring talent to the forefront while supporting causes considered important. The project is an extension of Aikaz Showroom, founded in 2010 by Tanya Sheikh with the goal of supporting talented designers with unique outlooks on design.

Personnel of New York

Address: 9 Greenwich Avenue
New York, NY 10014
Website: http://personnelofnewyork.com/

Personnel of New York opened it's doors in May of 2013, aiming to provide the neighborhood with a casual shop where inquisitive, like-minded New Yorkers could discover the work of emerging designers, artful creators, and independent brands.
Personnel is a reflection of the bohemian, artistic legacy of its West Village neighborhood and showcases women's fashion, footwear, jewelry, apothecary, and home goods from independent designers, colleagues, and friends from across the globe.
